How It Works

Three steps to
spatial sound.

01

Open & Scan

Point your iPhone at the floor. ARKit detects horizontal planes and anchors them to real-world coordinates with centimetre precision.

02

Place Zones

Tap to drop a glowing audio zone. Set the radius, assign a track from your library, and pick a colour. Stack as many as you like.

03

Move & Hear

Vision AI tracks people at 60 fps. Volume follows a cubic falloff — pure silence outside, full immersion at the centre.

Technology

Built on the
latest AR stack.

🧠

Vision AI Body Tracking

VNDetectHumanBodyPoseRequest tracks joint positions at 60 fps. Falls back through ankle → knee → root so detection survives partial occlusion.

🔊

RealityKit Spatial Audio

AudioFileResource attaches directly to AnchorEntity nodes. RealityKit's HRTF engine handles 3D spatialization — no extra math needed.

📐

Cubic Volume Falloff

Volume = (1 – d/r)³, smoothed at α = 0.09 per frame. It mirrors how acoustic energy naturally rolls off through physical space.

🌍

Multi-Zone Soundscapes

Scatter unlimited zones, each with its own track. Create spatial narratives — a different story in every corner of the room.
